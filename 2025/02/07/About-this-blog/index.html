# About

EN

Welcome to my little corner of the internet, where I share stories about life and technology. I created this blog for two reasons: first, to write short technical articles and document my learning journey; second, to explore a new way of expressing myself and record the internal dialogues that frequently run through my mind. These deep feelings are often difficult to articulate clearly when talking with friends and loved ones, and I hope that by writing them down, I can better present and organize my thoughts.
